# Financial Mobilization Strategy

**Status:** Open for contributions  
**Labels:** financial, transboundary  
**Assigned:** (to be assigned)

## Objective
Identify and detail potential funding sources (e.g., climate funds, carbon markets, public-private partnerships) for the West African Sustainable Forest Corridor.

## Background
Adequate and sustainable financing is critical for implementing forest conservation, restoration, and sustainable management activities across the region.

## Requested Input
We invite finance experts, donor agencies, and private sector representatives to contribute ideas and concrete proposals for mobilizing resources.

## Deliverable
A comprehensive financial mobilization strategy document.

## Current Draft
A draft outline has been proposed in [Financial_Mechanisms_Proposal.md](../Financial_Mechanisms_Proposal.md) (see Pull Request #1). Contributors are encouraged to expand and refine this outline.

## How to Contribute
1. Review the draft proposal and comment on the pull request.
2. Suggest additional funding mechanisms, case studies, or financial instruments.
3. Submit pull requests with detailed sections or revisions.

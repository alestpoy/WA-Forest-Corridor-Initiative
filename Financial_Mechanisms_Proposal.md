# Financial Mobilization Strategy – Draft Outline

## 1. Climate Finance & Carbon Markets
- Access international climate funds (Green Climate Fund, Adaptation Fund)
- Develop regional carbon‑credit projects under REDD+ and voluntary carbon standards
- Establish a West African Forest Carbon Trust to pool and manage carbon revenues

## 2. Public‑Private Partnerships (PPPs)
- Create blended‑finance vehicles that combine public seed capital with private investment
- Offer tax incentives and guarantees for sustainable forestry enterprises
- Partner with impact investors and development finance institutions (DFIs)

## 3. Innovative Financing Instruments
- Issue green bonds specifically earmarked for forest‑corridor restoration
- Set up payment‑for‑ecosystem‑services (PES) schemes at national and transboundary levels
- Explore debt‑for‑nature swaps with bilateral creditors

## 4. Capacity‑Building & Technical Assistance
- Secure dedicated funding for capacity building of forest administrations and local communities
- Leverage technical‑assistance windows of multilateral banks (World Bank, AfDB)
- Establish a regional fund for community‑based forest‑management grants

## Next Steps
- Quantify funding gaps per country and per intervention type
- Draft a detailed investment prospectus for potential donors
- Organize a regional donor‑roundtable to present the strategy
